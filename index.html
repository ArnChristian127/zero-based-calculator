STI Zero-Based
Calculator

The purpose of this calculator is to calculate the total grades for a specific subject by taking into account various factors such as assignments, quizzes, exams, participation,and other components that contribute to the overall grade. It allows students or educators to input individual scores or weightings for each component, and provides an accurate total grade based on these inputs. This tool is designed to assist in tracking academic performance and ensuring that all grading criteria are considered in the final grade calculation.
